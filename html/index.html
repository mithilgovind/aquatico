About Us


The OrangemelonGroup comprises of the restaurant business of acclaimed chef, restaurateur, TV personality and author Gordon Ramsay. It employs more than 700 people in London where it has a collection of 14 restaurants. The Group has a total of 29 restaurants globally and 6 Michelin stars, with international restaurants from Europe to the US and the Middle East. Originally routed in fine dining, the Group has expanded to include more casual fine dining sites offering a wide variety of guest experiences around the globe, such as Maze, Pétrus and Union Street Café in London, Gordon Ramsay Steak in Las Vegas, Gordon Ramsay at Forte Village, Sardinia, as well as the newly opened Bread Street Kitchen & Bar, Hong Kong, a concept taken from the Group’s Bread Street Kitchen, St Paul’s, London. Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea is the Group's flagship venue, which has held three Michelin-stars for more than 12 years.
